About Us
Norwich is a boutique firm that focuses on SPAC sponsoring, fund-raising, SPAC listing support services, and M&A and IPO support services. Norwich offers value creation through hands-on strategic and operational advisory services to mid-market or emerging growth companies whether through potential mergers, acquisitions, share purchases or exchanges, recapitalisation, reorganisation, restructuring, capital injections, and IPOs. Norwich is headquartered in Hong Kong, with a presence in China, Japan, and Malaysia.
