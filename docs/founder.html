Jason Wong
Founder
Our Founder

“ASIA's SPAC Godfather"

– Bloomberg News, March 2022
Founder and Head of Norwich, pioneer of Asian SPAC listing in the United States, with over 25 years of experience in the capital market.
  • Establishing Asia’s Maiden US-listed SPAC
    Jason is well known for his association with numerous high-profile, prolific US-listed SPACs. In 2014, he founded DT Asia Investments Limited (NASDAQ: CADTU), which was Asia’s very first US-listed SPAC following the 2008 financial crisis. More recently, Jason became the sponsor of Tottenham Acquisitions Limited (NASDAQ: TOTA) which merged with biotech company Clene Nanomedicine Inc in December 2020.
  • Building opportunities with small and
    medium Japanese companies.
    Outside of SPACs, Jason is the co-founder of Whiz Partners Asia Ltd. (‘Whiz Asia’), a private equity fund with notable ventures including listed companies Albert Inc., Advanced Media, Inc., CRI Middleware Co. Ltd., Digital Hearts Holdings Co. Ltd., and Shobunsha Publications, Inc., among many others.
  • Collaboration with Sony Interactive Entertainment
    One of Whiz Partners Asia’s largest forays to date is China Hero Fund, a partnership formed between Whiz Partners Asia Ltd and Sony Interactive Entertainment. China Hero Fund is the very first Chinese-funded premium game development project designed to propel homegrown Chinese-developed games into the international gaming spotlight.
